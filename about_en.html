About Under Control. 1156.

Along of the last 12 years, the arrival of July month for a Under Control’s members, marks the beginning of a fight and a dream achievement of being able to represent our school, Novo Hamburgo City and Brazil, participating of the biggest robotics competition in the world: the FIRST Robotics Competition, that occurs annually in regional editions at United States, Canada, Israel, and in a final step again at United States.

More than a robotic competition, participate of FIRST ROBOTICS COMPETITION is the possibility of achieving a dream, literally materialized in the robot conception, along of 6 weeks, witch following what determines FIRST, should accomplish tasks in a game always annualy released in january.

Furthermore, the competition envolves a lot more tha only the construction of a robot. Envolves determination, criativity, responsability, team spirits and, above all, believe that goals are idealized to be reached.

The Project of Robot Competition of FIRST represents a true challange for the students, involving activities that gather pleasure and a game motivation , with the responsability of a real work. In the project, search for make the youngs develop important abilities for their professional future, such as: have responsabilities, establish goals, live together with differences and work in team. The student has the oppotunity of live a challenge very close of a real professional work.

The team Under Control aim boosting the cience and tecnology in the country, through the robotic. Using the FIRST Robotics Competition as the form to cative the students and motive them to learn more about de area, presenting the for one of the forms more funnies and interectives of the science, that will make a difference in future of the whole society.

The robot is just the vehicle to the students learn a lot more about overcome dificulties and know how to solve problems either. Give opportunity for be understood in the practice the importance of the organization in a team and the power of sinergy in a group.
