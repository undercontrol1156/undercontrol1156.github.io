Under Control performs social actions all over the community, visiting places that have as public the lacking people of the region.

Learn More »

We are the team Under Control 1156, founded in 2002

at Marista Pio XII school of Novo Hamburgo – RS, Brasil. Every year we take part in the world’s biggest robotics competition, the FIRST Robotics Competition, in the United States. To achieve that, we count on support from sponsors, from Novo Hamburgo City Hall, from Marista Pio XII School and from the local community. In this competition, we have a deadline of six weeks to build the robot, which have to perform tasks that changes yearly. To learn more, click on the button below.

2014 Season. Achievements.

During the competition, Under Control saw the results of a whole building season materialize. The team conquered the Entrepreneurship Award, an award dedicated to the team which innovated at reaching people and fulfilling your goals. The award of Regional Winner was achieved by the team and its alliance that overcome all the others 50 teams of the regional. Under Control also conquered the “Innovation in Control Award sponsored by Rockwell Automation” for It’s innovator and functional control system, further the award of Regional Finalist.

For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology. FIRST.

The FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is an international engineering competition that involves teams from schools in more than 45 American states and 9 countries. The competition is intended to students of the high school, the robots’ “mentors” that work with the assistance from teachers, engineers, coaches, companies from the private sector, universities and interested people from the community who believe on the project.
