2015 Season, achievements.

While participating on the Long Island and Virginia regionals, Under Control saw the results not only from a building season, but from a whole year working towards FIRST, our team, our sponsors and our comunnity, materializing.





In Virginia, already in the regional’s first day, the team conquered the Team Spirit Award, which celebrates extraordinary enthusiasm and spirit through exceptional partnership and teamwork furthering the objectives of FIRST. Furthermore, we got 1st place on the qualification rounds, posteriorly reaching the semifinals.



In the Long Island regional, we reached again a good position in the qualification round and we had, for the second year in a row, the honor of being 329’s alliance partner, team which have been giving us help before, during and after the competition, thus reaching the semifinals with us. On account of a good and continuous work, accomplished throughout the year, we have been rewarded with the Chairman’s Award achievement, which honors the team that best represents a model for other teams to emulate and best embodies the purpose and goals of FIRST.



On account of Chairman’s Award achievement, we guaranteed a chance of taking part on the FRC World Festival, competition’s worldwide edition. In the momente, the team fights against the time in search of new sponsors and investors for being able to travel again to the United States and partipating on the Championship, in Saint Louis.
